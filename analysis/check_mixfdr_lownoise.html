Check mixfdr in low noise simulation

Matthew Stephens

2015-10-26

Last updated: 2015-11-18

Code version: 0a8454a78e79cf648ddfb8fd1fe7ca7a432ec35f

Here we check that mixfdr actually does OK at fitting the bimodal distribution when noise is small compared with bimodality.

First simulate bimodal data with well-separated peaks. The histogram shows the multi-modal nature of the underlying g is clear.

  hist(sim.bimodal$input$betahat,nclass=20,xlim=c(-10,10),prob=TRUE,main="histogram of simulated betahat values")

Now run mixfdr on it. Note that the fit captures the multi-modal nature of g, although mixfdr continues to overestimate pi0.
